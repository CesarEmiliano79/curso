Bienvenido a la serie Bike Race

This is a web page for the new Bike Race Series.



Bike Race is a new thrilling competitive event thaht allows cyclists to experience the excitement of HIGH-SPEED RACING on a scenic route without the risk (and cost) of traditional road racing.

Competitors will be timed during each category will be used to determine the winner of that category.
